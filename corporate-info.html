Our History

Since our founding in 1985, Air Jordan has been committed to delivering the finest sneakers that blend style, comfort, and performance. Our journey started with a passion for basketball and a vision to create timeless classics that resonate with enthusiasts worldwide.

Mission Statement

At Air Jordan, our mission is to inspire self-expression and confidence through our iconic sneakers. We aim to celebrate the cultural impact of basketball and provide a piece of history to sneaker enthusiasts around the globe.
